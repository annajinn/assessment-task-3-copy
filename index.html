    Our Cookies

    Crisp on the outside and a moist chewy centre that melts in the mouth, a combination of delightful textures originating from this sweet snackful dessert. Our cookie recipes vary with different flavours and toppings to suit and challenge your tastebuds. The recipe was tested over and over again, to perfect its qualities for the satisfaction of our customers as the ingredients and instruments utilized in the process are to their maximised quality.

    The name of the cookie originates from the Dutch term koekje, which implies "little cake." The first cookie was made in Persia in the 7th century A.D., when sugarcane had first been harvested. Every country appears to have a favourite: chocolate chip in North America, shortbread in the United Kingdom, sables and macarons in France, and biscotti in Italy.

    Our Bingsu

    Bingsu (alternative: Bingsoo) is a popular Korean shaved ice dessert with a variety sweet toppings that are specifically picked to match the overall taste combination that will satisfy our custromers. The original shaved ice flavour has been upgraded to shaved milk, a trending flavour that has a creamy yet icey texture.

    Bingsu is popular in numerous countries, originating from Korea, seen in countries like Thailand, China and Japan. This treat is perfect to share amongst friends and family, especially during the hotter seasons to cool down your body.

    Our Drinks

    There is nothing better than having our large variety of refreshing drinks that will beat the heat. These vibrant sweet drinks highlight the beauty of natural ingredients that include fruits and other products. The natural essence create a gorgeous collaboration with the base of the drink, ade as one of our popular bases in the series.

    Ade is a suffix used to denote a sweetened, fruit– (often citrus) flavored beverage, commonly served at Anna's cafe, as well as other drinks like ice coffees, ice chocolates, flavoured lattes and more. Drinks vary all around the globe, contrasting in flavours, textures, temperature and more, as our drinks specifically focus on the cool drinks that we prefer on hot days.
