    • FAQ

    Are walk-in purchases available?

    ↓

    We encourage our customers to purchase goods in person for the best variety of updated goods, though with COVID-19, circumstances have changed and lockdown has created difficulties for many people to come in.

    We accept walk-ins only on some days of the week. We also have delivery and pick-ups

    View our walk-in opening hours below to learn when you can visit us in person!

    Click here for opening hours
    Can someone else pickup for me?

    ↓

    Yes, this is allowed to happen, though it must happen under consent and proof from the original customer

    If it is a pick-up order, then send a confirmation email or phone call to let us now, providing their details (phone number, email, name).

    How can we order from your cafe?

    ↓

    We have three options on how to order

  • Walk-ins: Walk-ins are our most common type of ordering process which is simple for both you and us. We encourage contactless payment due to COVID-19.
  • Pick-up: Contact us on our social media sites that are located at the bottom of the page to secure a pick-up order. We will arrange and date and time for you to come to our store and pick-up your order, with only online payment available.
  • Delivery: Delivery is only accessable by phone call with one of our staff members so that there will be no delay of notices. You will recieve a text message from our postman when it arrives at your front door, with contactless payment.
    • Who do we contact for questions?

    ↓

    You can contact us via our contact options at the bottom of our page. Here is how you can contact us:

  • Instagram/Facebook: Send us a dm about a question or request and we will try our best to answer you as fast as we can, in under 24hrs.
  • Phone: Ring our number and our staff will answer any questions you have, though keep in mind that we will not be picking up any calls that aren't in our opening hours time frame.
  • Email: Send us an email about anything and we will try our best to get back to you and answer your question in 24hrs
    • What's the notice time delivery?

    ↓

    Notice period is required for both delivery and pick-ups

    Weekdays: A minimum of 2 days notice is required for us to process, make and package your order, while also being subject to our product availablities at the time.

    Weekends: A minimum of 4 days notice is required for us to process, make and package your order, while also being subject to our product availablities at the time.
