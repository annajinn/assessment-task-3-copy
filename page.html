    • Our Cookies

    Our bakers specialise in s'mores cookies to create a sweet taste and fluffy texture that contribute to the mouth-watering appearance. The combination of the popular campfire treat, s’mores, is paired perfectly with the textures of the cookie and additional flavours that create a sweet smoky flavour. S'mores are traditionally cooked over a campfire, although they can also be made at home over the flame of a wood-burning fireplace, in an oven, over a stove's flame, in a microwave, with a s'mores-making kit, or made in a panini press. The process of baking is undergone in the oven to create a golden crust on the exterior of the cookie and the marshmallow.

    The base ingredients are:
  • Butter (fat)
  • Sugar (brown and caster
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Plain flour
  • Chocolate (Dark, Milk & White)
