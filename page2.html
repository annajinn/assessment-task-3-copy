    Galaxy Ade

    This galaxy lemonade is as beautiful as it is tasty. Unlike many other colourful drinks that include artificial ingredient colourings, this one does not and rather uses fresh fruits to achieve the beautiful gradient that people love. The fruits that are utilised are fruits that are quite high in natural sugar to adjust the sweetness of the ade.

    • Jam Tea

    Most tea drinkers prefer adding sugar to their tea, well it's time to try it with jam? Jam enhances the natural flavours of tea without being overly sweet, making it a good substitute for sugar or honey. It also reduces bitterness and adds texture, and it derives from the Russian tradition of inserting huckleberry jam in tea boxes.

    Our flavours include:
  • Lemon
  • Blackberry
  • Raspberry
